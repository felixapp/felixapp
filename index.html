Don't Kill Felix!

Keep your little buddy healthy by walking more every day.

Felix is a game of life or death: you exercise, or you kill Felix. The star of the app is a digital pet whose health is in your hands. Felix integrates with Apple HealthKit to track your steps throughout the day and rewards your activity with food. Food is used to keep Felix happy and healthy, otherwise - to belabor the point - he will starve and die. We hope Felix helps users stay aware of their daily activity and provides a stimulus to get up and move!

Felix is a behavioral health intervention aimed at combating the terrifying and costly obesity epidemic.
