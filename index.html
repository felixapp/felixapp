Don't Kill Felix!

He's adorable. Please don't kill him.

Felix is a game of life and death. You walk, Felix lives. You sit, he dies. The app integrates with Apple HealthKit to track your steps throughout the day and rewards your activity with food for Felix. Walk a little bit more each day to earn the food he needs. Felix is cute and just wants to be loved. Please don't kill him.
